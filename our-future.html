SP70 Milestones & Achievements

Year Milestone Description
1954 Foundation of Singapore Polytechnic Singapore Polytechnic was established as Singapore's first polytechnic, aiming to provide technical education and training.
1984 Expansion of Campus The campus was expanded to include new facilities and academic departments to cater to growing student numbers.
2004 Introduction of New Programs New programs and courses were introduced to align with industry needs and technological advancements.
2014 60th Anniversary Celebrations Singapore Polytechnic celebrated its 60th anniversary with various events and initiatives reflecting on its achievements.
2024 70th Anniversary Milestones Celebrating 70 years of excellence in education, with new initiatives and global partnerships.
