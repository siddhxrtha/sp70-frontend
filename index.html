School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE)

The School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE) has a rich history tracing back to 1957. Today, we are Singapore's largest engineering school.
School of Architecture & the Built Environment (ABE)

Welcome to the School of Architecture & the Built Environment (ABE), where imagination is your greatest asset. Since 1958, ABE has been a leading institution in architecture and the built environment.
School of Business (SB)

At the School of Business (SB), we are committed in nurturing you to achieve success in your chosen paths! You have the power to shape your own learning experience.
School of Chemical & Life Sciences (CLS)

The School of Chemical & Life Sciences (CLS) has been a vital part of Singapore's industrial transformation and technological advancement.
Media, Arts & Design School (MAD)

Imagine a world where your creative ideas are nurtured, and your talents are honed. The Media, Arts & Design School is where your creative journey begins.
School of Life Skills & Communication (LSC)

LSC anchors the foundational modules taken by all SP students. The school is committed to develop critical & creative thinkers, confident communicators and self-directed learners ready for life, work and the world.
School of Mechanical and Aeronautical Engineering (MAE)

The School of Mechanical & Aeronautical Engineering (MAE) traces its roots back to 1958 when the first Technician Diploma in Mechanical Engineering was launched in Singapore.
School of Mathematics & Science (MS)

MS offers programmes to support students and adult learners to excel in mathematics, analytics, statistics, sciences and IT in engineering, technology and business courses.
Singapore Maritime Academy (SMA)

Embrace the ocean's call and embark on this voyage of learning at the Singapore Maritime Academy (SMA).
